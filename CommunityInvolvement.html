Community Involvement

One of my favorite places I served on many occasions is The Provo Bicycle Collective. I was able to use my bicycle mechanic skills to help serve the community of Provo by fixing up bikes that would be sold at a reduced cost or given away.

From 2018-2020 I was able to serve a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day-Saints in Anaheim California. While in California, I proselyted and served at many different non-profit organizations.
